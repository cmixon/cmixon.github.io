Data Visualization for FISH and Molecular Tests at NeoGenomics

Christopher Mixon, M.D., Medical Director, NeoGenomics Laboratories, Nashville, TN

The links below use idiograms to show chromosome location for each gene/probe in its respective NeoGenomics test group and to provide reference information and external links for more information about each gene. Use either the Gene Locator to find the chromosome location for a gene of interest and to see the ortholog location for another species (default Mus musculus), or the searchable Gene List file.
